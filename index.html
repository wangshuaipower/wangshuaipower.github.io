About Me

My name is Shuai Wang. Now, I am pursuing Ph.D in Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology. I received my bachelor degree from School of Computer Science, Harbin Institute of Technology in 2008. I worked as a research intern in Mobile and Sensing Systems Group at Mircosoft Research Asia, working with Xiaofan (Fred) Jiang (2011-2012). I have another internship in Intel Labs China, working with Xiaofan (Fred) Jiang (2013-2014). On the other side, I am also the cofounder and CTO of Air Scientific.

My main research interest lies in Wireless and Mobile Sensing Systems, Cyber-Physical Systems, Data Science and Big Data Analytics Platform. Now, I am working on the AirCloud project. We approach the challenging problem of accurate and affordable PM2.5 monitoring from a novel cloud-based data analytics perspective, and do the data analytics based on the dense deployment.
